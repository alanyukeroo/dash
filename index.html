How We Work with Clients

Considering the unique needs and requirements of each client, we develop customized training modules. To support this, we work closely with you to customize Selling Training Modules and methodologies. Generally, we follow a 6-step process:

01. Initial Contact

Through an initial phone conversation, we learn about your background, exchange information, and agree to a face-to-face meeting.

02. Meeting & Getting to Know Each Other

In this meeting, we establish the objectives of your project, how to measure its success, and its value to your company. A conceptual agreement is established with the main objective of getting to know you and your business.

03. Assessment

We assess the current gap in the selling skills of the client organization.

04. Training Proposal

The training module and methodologies will be presented for consideration and acceptance, along with timelines, terms, costs, and conditions.

05. Training Delivery

Consists of Classroom Training (Presentation of Training Material, Deep internalization in small groups, Role Play, and workshops) and Field Training.

06. Review and Follow Up

We conduct reviews and follow up to ensure the success of the training and implementation.
